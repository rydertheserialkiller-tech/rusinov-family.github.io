THE DUST

The Al'Arabawy bloodline was forged in the unforgiving desert borderlands. Unlike those born into privilege, the family began in poverty, where survival was the only law. In this harsh environment, Ryder Al'Arabawy learned that grit and loyalty were his only weapons.

"We didn't wait for the world to give us a chance; we took what was ours and built a throne from it."

Ryder's journey started in the shadows, working as a high-stakes bodyguard for the city's elite. He saved every cent to launch an investment firm, but the first chapter was a disaster—massive losses forced the doors to close. Ryder returned to his duty as a guard, undefeated in spirit.

Fate intervened when he met a legendary merchant in Los Santos. Recognizing Ryder's iron will, a partnership was born. Ryder reopened his empire, and today, Al'Arabawy stands as a dominant force in Wnash Time, controlling the market with precision and power.
