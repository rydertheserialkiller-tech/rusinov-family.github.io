OUR ROOTS

THE BLOODLINE

The Al'Arabawy family originated from the harsh, desert borderlands—a place where survival was a daily battle and poverty was the only shadow that followed you. Born into this unforgiving landscape, Ryder Al'Arabawy knew that the only way to escape the sand was through grit and blood.

"A man's worth isn't found in what he inherits, but in what he takes back from a world that gave him nothing."

His journey began in the shadows, working as a personal bodyguard for high-profile figures. Every bullet he dodged and every threat he neutralized was a lesson in power. He saved every cent to launch his first investment firm, but the city was cold. The business failed, leaving him bankrupt and forced back into his old life as a guard.

Destiny shifted when Ryder protected a tycoon from the elite circles of Los Santos. Impressed by his loyalty and iron will, the merchant offered him a seat at the table. Ryder didn't just take the seat; he rebuilt his empire, transforming a failed firm into a global syndicate that now dictates the market.
